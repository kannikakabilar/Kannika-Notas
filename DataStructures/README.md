34\. &ensp; Formula for summing 1+3+5+..+n <br>
    - nth term = 1 + 2*(n-1) <br>
    - sum total = n * (1 + nth term)/2 <br>
    - sum of 1+2+3+4+5+...+n = n*(n+1)/2 <br>
